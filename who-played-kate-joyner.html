She has since appeared in numerous films and television shows, including “Love Actually,” “Eragon,” “Resident Evil: Apocalypse,” and “Fortitude.” Guillory is known for her versatility as an actress, having played a wide range of roles throughout her career.

She has also received critical acclaim for her performances, including a nomination for a Screen Actors Guild Award for her role in the television miniseries “The Witches of Oz.”

In addition to her acting career, Guillory is also an advocate for various charitable causes, including animal rights and environmental conservation. She has worked with organizations such as the World Wildlife Fund and the Born Free Foundation to raise awareness and support for these causes.
