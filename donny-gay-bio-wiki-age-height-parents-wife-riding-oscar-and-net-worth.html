Donny Gay Biography

Donny Gay is an American one-time professional rodeo cowboy who specialized in bull riding. His father, Neal Gay, was a well-known rodeo competitor and later rodeo producer and stock contractor.

Don was established into the ProRodeo Hall of Fame in 1979; Neal was inaugurated in 1993, becoming the only father and son to receive that honor. In 2015, Don was established in the Bull Riding Hall of Fame.
