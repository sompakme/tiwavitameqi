Colombian actress, television host, model, and entrepreneur, Sofia Vergara, has an estimated net worth of $160 million.

Vergara was named television top earner, according to figures compiled by Forbes magazine. The prime time TV reigning queen out-earned reality star Kim Kardashian and Desperate Housewives’ Eva Longoria with $19 million between May 2011 and May 2012, all thanks to her role on the ABC Modern Family, endorsement and licensing deals; including a clothing line at KMart advertising campaigns for Diet Pepsi, Burger King and income from her media company LatinWE.

Sofia Vergara makes $65,000 per episode of the Modern Family. Discovered by a photographer while walking on a Colombian beach, Vergara began making her name in show business as a model at the age of 17, before transiting to TV hosting and acting.  Her hosting debut was the popular 1995 travel series, Fuera de Serie. Sofia Vergara's crossover appeal was cemented with her performance on the FOX Network's 1995 American Comedy Awards which launched her almost instantaneously into Hollywood stardom. As a sex symbol of the highest order, the busty Colombian is one of the sexiest women in show business.

Vergara was named AskMen's Most Desirable Woman of 2012. She currently stars on the critically acclaimed ABC series Modern Family as Gloria Delgado-Pritchett, for which she was nominated for a Golden Globe Award, two Primetime Emmy Awards, and three Screen Actors Guild Awards.
