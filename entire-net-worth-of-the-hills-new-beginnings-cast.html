Thirteen years after reality teen drama The Hills debuted on MTV, some of the original cast members reunited, and The Hills: New Beginnings, was born. Joining the alumni are newbies Mischa Barton, and Pamela Anderson and Tommy Lee's son, Brandon. Heidi and Spencer Pratt are celebrating 10 years of married bliss, Audrina is moving on in her career after a rough divorce, and Whitney Port is enjoying the balance between being a mom and wife, and a successful businesswoman.

Struggles with sobriety, relationship breakdowns, and family drama are proving to be the name of the game for the new series. So how much are these stars worth, exactly? Let's dive into the net worth of the top cast members.

Heidi Montag: $100,000

Though Heidi and Spencer made millions in the mid- 2000's when The Hills first got big, they have been candid about their history of overspending. Living large was the name of the game when they first emerged in Hollywood, and their money quickly slipped through their fingers.

Spending on everything from lavish crystals to plastic surgery, the couple are slowly rebuilding their fortune. With their son, Gunner, now in the picture, the pair are determined to make smarter choices with their expenses.

Spencer Pratt: $100,000

Spencer has notoriously been the big spender in the Speidi relationship, and he continues to have expensive taste. Jewelry, crystals, and private yacht rides are just some of the things that he has spent money on in the new season.

With the emergence of his crystal company, Pratt Daddy Crystals, and a return to the big screen, Spencer now has some guaranteed and steady income to balance out all of his spending. Hopefully he doesn't repeat his old bad habits, and he can finally establish a nice nest egg.

Audrina Patridge: $5 Million

Audrina Patridge is one of the original returning cast members of The Hills. This season focuses of the aftermath of her divorce, her experiences as a single mother, and her inevitable interest in ex-boyfriends Justin Bobby, and Ryan Cabrera.

She has been quite successful as a model, television presenter, and swimwear designer. This season on The Hills: New Beginnings, she relaunches her swimwear line, Prey, with a trendy outdoor fashion show and launch party. She is worth approximately $5 million.

Whitney Port: $5 Million

Whitney Port is another original cast member who has been successful in the fashion world since she was just out of high school. As an intern for Teen Vogue magazine, she made several connections in the fashion world and went on to work for notorious designer Diane von Furstenburg.

After the success of The Hills, she went on to star as the lead role in spinoff MTV show The City. She now runs a successful blog website, where she pr0motes wellness and fashion products. Her net worth is approximately $5 million.

Stephanie Pratt: $500,000

Stephanie Pratt is the little sister of Spencer Pratt and has returned to the new season of The Hills: New Beginnings with the intention of mending her estrangement with her brother and Heidi. After some dependence issues and legal issues, Stephanie got her life back on track, and landed a role on the popular British reality show Made In Chelsea.

She has appeared in several magazines as a model and launched her own collection for Goddiva in 2017. Her net worth is currently sitting close to $500,000 and continues to grow with her television exposure.

Brody Jenner: $10 Million

Brody Jenner is perhaps one of the most recognized alumni members of The Hills. The son of Linda Thompson and Caitlyn Jenner, he has been in the spotlight since he was a young child.

He has modeled for Guess, Agent Provocateur, and has appeared several times in the Cosmogirl magazine. Other than his appearances on The Hills and The Hills: New Beginnings, he has appeared in several episodes of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, though he does have a strained relationship with most of his stepsiblings and his former step-mother, Kris Jenner.

Mischa Barton: $1 Million

Mischa Barton rose to fame in 2003, when she played Marisa Cooper in cult classic teen TV show The O.C. After a hiatus from Hollywood, Mischa returned to the spotlight as a supporting cast member on The Hills: New Beginnings. 

The first season has been focusing on her return to acting, her complicated relationship with her mother, and her newfound independence and confidence as an actor in Hollywood. Her net worth currently sits at around $1 million, and will likely go up in the coming years as she appears in more films and TV shows.

Justin Brescia: $150,000

Justin Brescia, aka Justin Bobby, is most known for being Audrina's on-again off-again love interest in the original The Hills series. The pair seem to have returned to old habits when they tried to go on a few dates early in the season. Justin has since moved on, and has an official girlfriend, but he and Audrina have tried to repair their friendship and are on better terms.

He has worked as a model and a musician, and had a professional hair styling product line from 2012-2014, called BRUSH by Justin Bobby Brescia. His net worth is about $150,000.

Brandon Thomas Lee: $1 Million

Brandon Thomas Lee is the son of rocker Tommy Lee and actress Pamela Anderson. At just 23 years old, he is the youngest cast member, although he has a maturity that is beyond his years.

He is often the one giving reasonable advice to Spencer and Brody. The Hills: New Beginnings is his first television debut; though he is an aspiring actor, and hoping to land a lead film role soon. Most of his net worth comes from funds that he acquired from his successful parents. He has a current net worth of about $1 million.

Ryan Cabrera: $2 Million

Ryan Cabrera appeared on several episodes of the original The Hills, when Audrina started dating him casually in 2010. Almost a decade later, and the pair seem to be repeating history. Ryan is best known for his solo singing career.

Though he hasn't released a studio album since 2008, he has released several singles in the last few years, including his most recent, "Inside Your Mind". Ryan has a net worth of about $2 million.
