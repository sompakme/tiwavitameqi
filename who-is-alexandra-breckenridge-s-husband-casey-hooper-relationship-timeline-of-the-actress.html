Who Is Alexandra Breckenridges Husband: Casey Hooper? Relationship Timeline Of The Actress

American actress Alexandra Breckenridge is married to Casey Hooper, who is the lead guitarist of Katy Perry. The couple has been married for almost seven years now.

Alexandra Breckenridge is most famously known for her portrayal of Jessie Anderson in the AMC series The Walking Dead and Sophie in the NBC series This is Us. Also, she has starred in the Netflix series Virgin River as Melinda “Mel” Monroe.

Apart from acting, Alexandra is also a voice actor who has voiced various characters in the comedy series Family Guy. She started her career with supporting roles in the teen comedy movies Big Fat Liar and She’s the Man.

Who Is Alexandra Breckenridge’s Husband Casey Hooper?

Casey Hooper is the husband of Alexandra Breckenridge. He is a guitarist who is associated with the likes of Katy Perry, Steve Perry, and Surfmaster.

Hooper is an ordinary person who rose to fame as the lead guitarist of the American mega-star Kety Perry. He is often seen with the singer in her live acts and has been an ever-present figure in her performances.

The guitarist has performed with Perry in various high-profile venues and events such as the halftime show at Super Bowl XLIX and the Grammy Awards Ceremony. His charm and unique style of playing has made millions of fans worldwide.

Casey was born in Des Moines, Iowa to Dave Hooper and Rebecca Cartwright. Since his parents split when he was a kid, he was raised by his mother. Both of his parents are now married to other people. He has a brother Spencer who reportedly has Down’s Syndrome.

The Hoopers run a family business of an Action Warehouse, which was started by Casey’s paternal grandparents. He was gifted a Fender Stratocaster on his 13th birthday and has not looked back ere since,

On 2005, Casey moved to Los Angeles to pursue a musical career. He was hired by Perry in 2010 and has embarked on a successful musical journey with the Fireworks singer.

Alexandra Breckenridge And Casey Hooper Relationship Timeline

Alexandra Breckenridge married Casey Hooper on September 13, 2015, in Dunton Hot Springs, Colorado. The couple had been dating for some years before Alexandra announced her engagement on September 16, 2014.

The couple was blessed with their first child, a son named Jack, in September 2016. Later in December 2017, Alexandra gave birth to their second child, a daughter named Billie.

Breckenridge and Hooper have been featured on each other’s Instagram on several occasions. When they are not working, the couple is seen taking on adventures and traveling along with their children.

Alexandra Breckenridge And Casey Hooper Age Difference And Children

Alexandra Breckenridge and Casey Hooper are four years apart from each other. Actually, the wife is four years older than the husband.

Actress Alexandra Breckenridge was born on May 15, 1982, in Bridgeport, Connecticut. She is currently 40 years old. On the other hand, guitarist Casey Hooper was born on July 16, 1986, in Des Moines, Iowa. He is currently 36 years old.
