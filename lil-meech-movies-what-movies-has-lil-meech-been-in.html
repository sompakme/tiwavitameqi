Lil Meech Movies: What Movies Has Lil Meech Been In?

Lil Meech has starred in the movies Black Mafia Family (2021), Euphoria (2022), and Taurus (2022).

In Black Mafia Family, he played the role of his father Demetrius Flenory Snr.

The 50 cent adaptation series follows the true story of two Detroit brothers, Demetrius “Big Meech” and Terry “Southwest T” Flenory, who are Lords of a drug cartel.

They become the most influential but criminal families in America. They were well known for their uncommonly extravagant lifestyles.

Demetrius “Big Meech” set up BMF entertainment, a hip-hop music business that promoted several high-profile hip-hop artists at the time.

The business was funded with money obtained from the sale of cocaine and other hard drugs.

The series was set in Atlanta, and Lil Meech eventually played his father’s role because it was difficult finding an actor for the role.

Lil Meech described the experience as “uneasy” as he had enrolled in acting classes in order to fit in properly.

Demetrius “Big Meech” Flenory had also followed up with his son, and creator Randy Huggins throughout the whole process of the movie production.

The crime drama series has received approval for a second season.
