Uptown restaurant 204 North Kitchen &amp; Cocktails closes for renovation WSOC TV

CHARLOTTE — Uptown restaurant 204 North Kitchen & Cocktails has temporarily closed as part of plans for a whirlwind facelift.

The décor will be refreshed and the menu updated as part of those efforts. The fine-casual dining space will have the same vibe — and even better food and drinks, says Anthony Kearey, operating partner for Mason Jar Group.

The Charlotte hospitality company is behind Tilt on Trade in uptown, The Union in South End, Grace O’Malleys in Matthews and Bargarita in NoDa.

The goal it to reopen 204 North on Aug. 24.
