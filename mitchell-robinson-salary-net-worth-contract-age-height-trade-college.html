Mitchell Robinson Salary, Net Worth, Contract, Age, Height, Trade, College

Mitchell Robinson is an American professional basketball player for the New York Knicks of the National Basketball Association.

Mitchell Robinson III was born on April 1, 1998, in Pensacola, Florida, the United States to his mother, Lakesha Robinson.

Mitchell Robinson attended Pine Forest High School in Pensacola, Florida for the first two years of his high school career. He later transferred to Landry-Walker College and Career Preparatory High School in New Orleans as a sophomore but he never played there.

Mitchell Robinson has first appeared in the summer league before his junior year for Chalmette High School. He had enrolled at Western Kentucky University for the summer semester in July 2017.

On June 21, 2018, Mitchell Robinson was selected with the 36th overall pick in the 2018 NBA draft by the New York Knicks.
