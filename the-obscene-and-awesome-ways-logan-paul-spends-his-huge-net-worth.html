The Obscene And Awesome Ways Logan Paul Spends His Huge Net Worth

American YouTuber, boxer, internet personality, and actor Logan Paul has a massive net worth of $45 million. With this money, it is not surprising that he lives a rather extravagant life. Throughout his YouTube career, he has been named one of the highest-paid YouTubers in the world. However, with the additional millions of wealth coming into the YouTuber, he inevitably continues living a lavish life. He is known to spend his money grandly, and here are some of the excessive purchases of Logan Paul using his massive fortune.

8 The YouTuber Turned Boxer Bought A Couch Worth $90,000

Logan has made terrible choices in his life; however, according to him, he only has two regrets, and one of his biggest regret is buying his $90,000 couch. He purchased the hideous couch for his mansion located in L.A. The sofa is a crescent-shaped Mercedes-Benz sofa that can comfortably seat about ten people and can be configured according to the owner's liking.

As Logan redesigns his L.A. mansion, he believes the Couch does not fit in his home anymore and decided to sell it instead. He put up an ad on Craigslist for the Couch and only sold it for $20,000.

7 Logan Paul Spent $3.5 Million On Pokemon Cards

Logan Paul has admitted that he fancies strange and luxurious items. The YouTuber does not hesitate to spend his millions whenever he likes something. One of these unusual purchases is when he bought the first edition of Pokémon cards for $3.5 million. Unfortunately for Logan Paul, it seems like he could not catch them all as he later found out that the cards were fake.

6 Logan Paul Dropped $230,000 On A Mercedes Benz G550 4X4

Mercedes Benz G550 is the most luxurious car Logan Paul has purchased. 5 The Maverick Bought A Highly Coveted $5.2 Million Pokemon Card

Logan made a grand entrance on his debut at the WrestleMania wearing a Pokémon card. Upon entering the arena, the YouTuber wore the most highly coveted Pikachu graphic card as a necklace and paired it with Wolverine-inspired black and yellow costume. The Pikachu Illustrator is among the rarest cards in the Pokemon world.

The Internet personality dropped $5.2 million for the said Pokémon card and claims that only 39 cards were distributed and given out to the winners of an Illustration contest in 1998. Additionally, the card he owns is the only one in the world that was graded 10, which indicates its perfect condition.
