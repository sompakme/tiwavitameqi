Zambada’s Criminal Activities and Operations

Zambada’s organization, the Sinaloa Cartel, is heavily involved in drug trafficking operations, primarily focusing on cocaine and heroin. The cartel utilizes a wide range of methods and transportation modes to smuggle drugs into the United States.

These methods include:

  • Aircraft: The cartel frequently utilizes private planes and small aircraft to transport drugs across international borders. These aircraft are often modified to carry large quantities of drugs while evading detection.
  • Narcosubs: Zambada’s cartel is known for its use of semi-submersible vessels, commonly referred to as narcosubs, which can travel just below the water surface to avoid detection by radar and law enforcement patrols.
  • Container ships: The Sinaloa Cartel also exploits legal trade routes, hiding drugs within legitimate cargo shipments aboard container ships. This method allows them to transport drugs in large quantities while leveraging global trading networks.
  • Go-fast boats: High-speed vessels, known as go-fast boats, are used for smuggling drugs along coastal routes, taking advantage of their speed and maneuverability to evade law enforcement.
  • Fishing vessels: The cartel often employs fishing boats as a cover for drug smuggling operations. These vessels blend in with legitimate fishing activities while transporting drugs hidden among the catch.
  • Buses, rail cars, tractor trailers, and automobiles: To distribute drugs within the United States, the Sinaloa Cartel uses various land transportation methods, including the use of buses, rail cars, tractor trailers, and automobiles. These modes allow for the discreet movement of drugs across states, reaching various distribution cells.

By smuggling large quantities of cocaine primarily from Colombian sources, the Sinaloa Cartel has established an extensive distribution network that reaches multiple states in the United States. Some of the major states include Arizona, Atlanta, California, Illinois, and New York.

“The Sinaloa Cartel’s drug trafficking operations encompass a vast array of transportation methods, enabling them to maximize profits and evade law enforcement. From aircraft and narcosubs to container ships and land-based vehicles, their operation is highly sophisticated.”- DEA Spokesperson
