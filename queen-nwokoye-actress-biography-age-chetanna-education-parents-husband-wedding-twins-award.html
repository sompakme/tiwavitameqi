Queen Nwokoye Career

In 2004, Nwokoye joined the Nigerian movie industry (Nollywood) and made her acting debut in the movie Nna Meen. The movie that brought her to limelight and ever since then, she has gone on to star in several Nigerian films. Queen has acted in over 250 Nollywood movies which include Nna Meen, His Majesty, The Girl is Mine, Odum Na Akwaeke, Security Risk, Adaure, Save The Baby, Chetanna, among others. She is also known for starring as the lead character in a 2014 popular film titled Chetanna. Nwokoye’s acting skills have encouraged a lot of movie lovers to watch more Nollywood films.
