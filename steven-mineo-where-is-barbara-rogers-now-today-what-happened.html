Steven Mineo: Where Is Barbara Rogers Now/Today What Happened?

In a bizarre incident in 2017, Barbara Rogers was accused of shooting her boyfriend with a pistol after he allegedly asked for it. Where is Barbara now? Continue reading to find out.

According to 911 operators, they received a girl calling after killing her boyfriend, Steven Mineo. The reports say that her reasoning for the murder was that he asked to be shot.

In Mount Pocono, Pennsylvania, they found the body of Steven Mineo, who was shot by a .45 pistol. After seeing the body of dead Steven, Barabara was taken into custody. Let us learn where she is now.

Where Is Barbara Rogers Now/Today – What Happened?

In 2017, Barbara was taken into custody for a possible murder. She called the authorities after shooting her boyfriend, and she said that her boyfriend asked to be shot at. 

And after being processed for more than two years, in 2019, she was charged with first-degree murder. After eliminating all the possibilities of it being a suicide, it was confirmed that Barbara killed Steven.

After the confirmation, the jury found her guilty of third-degree murder. She was sentenced to 15-40 years in prison, and according to reports, she is currently spending her time in prison.

What Happened To Steven Mineo And Barbara Rogers?

Steven and Barbara met in 2011 and fell in love because of the likeness of interests. The couple spent six years together before a bizarre incident separated the pair.

Barbara was diagnosed with Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) from her time in the army. She was already an army veteran when she met Steven.

As both were firm believers in the alien reptile cult, they hit it off from the first time they met each other.

On the day of the death of Steven, according to Barbara, he had a gun. He asked Barbara to hold a gun and press the trigger after bringing a gun into the house. She did not even know what she had done until after.

Steven Mineo Death Cause Explored
