Birthdate February 6, 1957 Birthplace Chicago, Illinois, USA Education Shirley Townsend, Ed Townsend Profession Comedian Net worth $2,000,000 Source of Wealth Comedy, Movie Directing, Screenwriting, Producing, Acting Nationality American Marital Status Married Cheri Jones (m. 1990–2001) Children Skye Townsend, Sierra Townsend, Grace Townsend, Isaiah Townsend

Actor Robert Townsend has a net worth of $2 million. The American actor started his career in the 70s where he would perform at local plays. He already had an innate passion for acting. He tried to finish college by enrolling in Illinois State University and later on in Negro Ensemble Company. However, he felt that studying took him away from his acting career. So, he dropped out and pursued a fulltime career in acting.

Hollywood Shuffle was Townsend’s first project. He wrote, produced, and directed the comedy film. The success of the film propelled him to fame and a good name in the industry. He also made other films, like The Five Heartbeats, Livin’ for Love: The Natalie Cole Story, and Carmen: A Hip Hopera among many others.

Townsend is also a writer, director, network programming CEO, and a producer. His two daughters Skye and Sierra Townsend are both in the industry, as well.
