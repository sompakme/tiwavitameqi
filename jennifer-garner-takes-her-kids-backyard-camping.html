Jennifer Garner Takes Her Kids Backyard Camping

Jennifer Garner isn’t a regular mom, she’s a cool mom — even if that means getting woken up by sprinklers while on an outdoor adventure with her kids!

“Backyard campout,” the Peppermint actress, 47, captioned a selfie of herself on Instagram that shows her under a sleeping bag on Saturday, June 29. “Kids still sleeping. Sprinklers. 😑”

Garner also added the hashtags #whencanihavecoffee and #chickensareloud, both of which sparked reactions from fellow moms — celebrities included.

“Probably the coolest mama ever,” Married at First Sight alum Jamie Otis wrote in the comments section. Added Bachelor Nation’s Ali Fedotowsky, “The hashtags!”

Garner — who shares daughters Violet, 13, and Seraphina, 10, and son Samuel, 7, with ex-husband Ben Affleck — is no stranger to making sure she fills their children’s lives with fun and adventures. The Alias alum even took Samuel and his boy scout troop camping in April.

“When you start a Saturday in New York and wake up Sunday at a California campground….you deserve/win/get? all the coffee,” Garner captioned an Instagram picture of herself cuddling with a Spider-Man pillow at the time.

The day prior, the pair were spotted laughing together while they attended one of their children’s soccer games. “Ben and Jen have immense love and respect for one another,” an insider told Us Weekly in October 2018. “They still coparent together and that will always come first.
