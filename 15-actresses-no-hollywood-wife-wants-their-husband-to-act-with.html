When we go out to watch a movie, hardly ever do we take time to think of the actors and actresses' personal lives. We simply pay attention to what is happening on screen, hoping everything works out the way we want. Professional actors and actresses have a duty to get into character, and often this involves forgetting everything they are and becoming everything their character is. Those who succeed in bringing their characters to life receive praise and accolades, and such success opens up new opportunities to further their careers.

Although everyone loves to be associated with a successful actor, especially close family and friends, a group of people in Hollywood suffers silently in the midst of success, and these are the wives and/or husbands of these celebrities. Regardless of how much you understand the business, and how much trust you've placed in your spouse, seeing him or her taking part in an intimate scene with another man or woman is bound to mess you up. The chemistry that characters share on stage and the lengths the directors often go to make the scenes look as real as possible are enough to spark jealousy and distrust even among the most powerful couples in Hollywood. Since marriages and relationships have ended because of one of the parties acting alongside talented and arguably better-looking co-stars, many Hollywood celebrity spouses are not always excited about the success of their significant other's films.

Since people believe that men are the weaker gender when it comes to cheating on their women, Hollywood wives often have to hope that their men will remember the commitment they made to them, especially when they're working. Here is a list of 15 actresses today's Hollywood wife would never want her husband to act alongside, especially if the script requires them to get intimate. Most of these women are young and single, and it's important to know that they can get any man they desire, and they would not need to go to great lengths to do so.
