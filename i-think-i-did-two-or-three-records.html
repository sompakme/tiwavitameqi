I Think I Did Two Or Three Records

Justin Timberlake‘s upcoming album, The 20/20 Experience, is looking like it’ll feature some familiar faces—like frequent collaborator T.I.

Tip spoke with MTV News to promote Identity Thief—his new film also starring Jason Bateman and Melissa McCarthy—but not without some JT talk. Here’s what he had to say about hooking back up in the studio with Justin:

“Yeah, I spoke to JT. Me, him and [Timbaland] was on the phone. I did a record for them at the end of last year. They were working on a record together, and I had the pleasure of assisting them on their particular record. I think it’s called ‘Goodbye Homey,’ one of them is, and then there is another one; I did maybe two or three records with them.”

Definitely sounds like an interesting track. We wonder if it’ll be more in the feel of “Dead & Gone” or the pop hit, “My Love.”
