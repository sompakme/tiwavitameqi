Henrik Zetterberg Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Zetterberg (born October 9, 1980) is a Swedish professional ice hockey centre/left wing who plays for the Detroit Red Wings of the National Hockey League. Zetterberg was named captain of the Red Wings in 2008. (NHL). In addition to winning the Stanley Cup with the Detroit Red Wings in 2008, Zetterberg was named the Conn Smythe Trophy as the Most Valuable Player of the Stanley Cup Playoffs that year. He also won gold medals in the 2006 Ice Hockey World Championships and the 2006 Winter Olympics, making him a member of the exclusive Triple Gold Club of ice hockey players.
