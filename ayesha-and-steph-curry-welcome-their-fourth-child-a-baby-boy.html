Ayesha And Steph Curry Welcome Their Fourth Child, A Baby Boy

Ayesha and Stephen Curry are officially a family a six.

Over Memorial Day weekend, the couple announced the arrival of their fourth child, a baby boy named Caius Chai. The Curry’s also shared that he was born earlier this month on May 11.

“Our sweet baby boy decided to make an early arrival!!” the pair captioned a joint post of the newborn holding one of their fingers on Instagram. “He’s doing great and we are finally settling in at home as a family of 6! So grateful!. Caius Chai 5/11/24.”

Just before Mother’s Day this year, Ayesha shared a photo of her hiking with their eight-year-old daughter Ryan. The two enjoyed the outdoor weather as Ryan gave her mom a huge kiss on the cheek.

“Hikes with my Chookas. My nature girl,” the 35-year-old captioned the precious photo.

Ayesha revealed she was expecting a new bundle of joy back in March for that month’s cover of her own magazine, Sweet July.

“For so many years, Stephen and I thought we were done,” she said in the digital issue. “We said, ‘Three, that’s it, we’re not doing this again.’ For me, the decision came from always finding myself looking around and feeling like somebody was missing. I would load up the car and think, ‘Oh, I forgot something.’ But nobody was forgotten. It started to turn my brain a little bit. Maybe somebody was missing. So we set out on this journey, knowing that this would complete our family.”

Caius joins his siblings Riley Elizabeth, 11, Ryan Carson, 8, and Canon W. Jack, 5, as the missing piece to the puzzle. Considering age and the concerns that comes with pregnancy in your 30’s, Ayesha added that her latest pregnancy with the NBA champ will be her last.

“But one of the biggest differences is a new understanding that this really does pass by in the blink of an eye. And in the blink of an eye, it could also all be taken away,” she said. “That’s encouraged me to slow down and take in every single little thing—especially with it being my last pregnancy.”
