About Bobby Lee

Bobby Lee, born on September 17, 1971, is an American stand-up comedian, actor, and podcaster. He gained recognition for his work on the sketch comedy show MADtv and has since continued to make a name for himself in the comedy world. He is the host of the popular podcasts TigerBelly and Bad Friends, where he engages in funny conversations with guests and provides comedic insights into life and entertainment.
