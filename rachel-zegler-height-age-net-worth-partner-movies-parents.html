Rachel Zegler Height, Age, Net Worth, Partner, Movies, Parents

American actress and singer, Rachel Ziegler, stands at 5 ft 1 in (157.0 cm) tall.

Rachel Zegler Age
Zegler was born on May 3, 2001. She is 20 years-old.

Rachel Zegler Net Worth
Ziegler has a net worth of approximately $3 million.

Rachel Zegler Partner
Zegler confirmed in February that she is dating her West Side Story co-star – Josh Andres Rivera.

Rachel Zegler Movies
Ziegler has featured in Legally Blonde, Cosette in Les Misérables, and Millie in Thoroughly Modern Millie.

Zegler will next star in the upcoming superhero film Shazam! Fury of the Gods.

She will also be playing Snow White alongside Gal Gadot as the Evil Queen in a live-action film adaptation of the 1937 animated Disney film.

Rachel Zegler Parents
Zegler was born to Gina and Craig Zegler. She was named after the fictional character Rachel Green from the TV show Friends. She has an older sister.

Her mother is of Colombian descent. Zegler’s maternal grandmother immigrated from Colombia to the United States in the 1960s. Her father is of Polish descent.
