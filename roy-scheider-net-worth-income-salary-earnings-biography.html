Roy Scheider Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Roy Richard Scheider (November 10, 1932 – February 10, 2008) was an American actor and amateur boxer who was born in the state of New York in 1932. In addition to his roles as Police Chief Martin C. Brody in the first two Jaws films, choreographer and film director Joe Gideon in All That Jazz, Detective Buddy “Cloudy” Russo in The French Connection, NYPD Detective Buddy Manucci in The Seven Ups (his first lead role), and Captain Nathan Bridger in the science fiction television series seaQuest DSV, he was also known for his supporting roles in the films The French Connection and The Seven Ups.  Scheider’s last performance was included in the 2011 thriller Iron Cross, which was released after his death. “One of the most distinctive and outstanding of all Hollywood performers,” according to AllMovie, Scheider has been nominated for two Academy Awards and has also been nominated for a Golden Globe Award and a BAFTA Award.
