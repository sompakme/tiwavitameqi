Perfect! Yuri achievement in Doki Doki Literature Club Plus!

If you go to the “Metaverse” fake desktop (by exiting DDLC in-game, NOT exiting to your xbox dashboard), you can open a folder marked “files”, then a folder marked “game”, then a file marked “poemwords.txt”. This will give you a list of Sayori's, Natsuki's and Yuri's favourite words to use in poems. Exclusively use words from the appropriate list to write a “perfect” poem.
