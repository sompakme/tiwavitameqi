Who Is Vinny Guadagnino Girlfriend? Sister and Father, Meet The Family

Fans felt that Vinny Guadagnino might find a girlfriend in Season 3 of Double Shot at Love. The family-oriented guy is very close to his sisters and family.

Guadagnino is an American reality television personality and author known for shows including Jersey Shore, Double Shot at Love, Jersey Shore: Family Vacation, and Revenge Prank. He appeared as Hammerhead in The Masked Dancer. 

Vinny launched IHAV, a clothing brand in 2011, and another clothing brand, Name Brand NYC, in 2018. He supports animal rescue organizations and is a supporter of gay rights.

Does Vinny Guadagnino Have A Girlfriend?

Vinny has dated several women in the past. While appearing in an interview with Kelly Osbourne and Jeff Beacher, he revealed that his relationship status is single. 

Guadagnino was previously linked to Ramona Singer in 2010. He dated Melanie Iglesias from 2012 to 2014 and Elicea Shyann from 2017 to 2018. 

He has never been married, and he does not have a wife. Vinny’s relationship with Alysee Joyner from Season 1 of Double Shot at Love did not work out.

He was seen with Francesca Farago from Too Hot to Handle in the summer of 2020. The pair were getting dinner which sparked the dating rumors. They were also seen flirting on Instagram after the date. 

Fans are hoping that Vinny finally finds himself some love on the Season 3 of Double Shot At Love. After several heartbreaks in the past, he was ready to change his ways.

Model Peyton Freestone and dancer Emily Piccinonno were hoping to secure a spot in Guadagnino’s heart.

Who Are Vinny Guadagnino Sisters?

Vinny has two siblings, both of whom are sisters. His sisters are Antonella Guadagnino and Mariann Guadagnino. 

They have not been as active on reality TV as Vinny or their mother, but the family seems to be very close. 

Guadagnino was essentially raised in a family of women, and he mostly spent time with his mother. As he did not come from money, he feels that his family is important than anything else.

Meet Vinny Guadagnino Father And Family

Vinny’s father is noticeably absent from the show. Talking about his dad’s lack of presence on the show, he revealed that he just never got involved, reports Distractify.

The reality star’s parents separated, and his father lived in the downstairs part of the family’s home. 

He grew up in Staten Island, New York, USA, with his family. Guadagnino comes from a traditional Italian-American family. Their family’s farm was visited on an episode in Jersey Shore’s Season 4.
