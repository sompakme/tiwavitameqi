Ryan Reynolds Tweets NSFW Difference Between Ryan Gosling and Himself

No need to play who’s who here! Ryan Reynolds took to Twitter on Thursday, June 25, to set the record straight about how he and fellow Hollywood heartthrob Ryan Gosling are not the same person — and he used some not-so-PG words to get his point across.

The new father (to 6-month-old daughter James, with wife Blake Lively) caught wind of an oblivious Twitter user’s offhanded comment, and decided to address her in a cheeky reply.

“I honestly don’t know the difference between ryan gosling and ryan reynolds,” @Stonedziti wrote late Wednesday. Several hours later, Reynolds, 38, replied with a NSFW tweet.

“Different hair colors,” the Deadpool actor quipped. “Ryan Gosling has light BLOND hair. And Ryan Reynolds is a c—t.”

The tone of the tweet may be surprising coming from Reynolds, who largely stays out of the limelight, but the jokester and Lively, 27, have both been known to tweet many tongue-in-cheek messages.

Just last month, Reynolds shared a shot of a man (presumably him) hunched over a motorcycle with the caption, “Don’t try this at home. Particularly, in the living room.”
