Is Francesca Eastwood Destined For Stardom?

It would be a lie to say that you are unaware of Clint Eastwood, the well-known American film director and actor. Her daughter, Francesca Eastwood, is a star in her own right, following in her father’s footsteps.

So, who is Francesca Eastwood and how much effort and hard work does she put into her professional career in order to be the finest in the Hollywood industry?

About Francesca Eastwood

Born on August 7, 1993, in Redding, California, Francesca Ruth Fisher-Eastwood is not only a tv personality but also a filmmaker, model, actress, and socialite.

She is the daughter of famous film director and actor, Clint Eastwood and actress, Frances Fisher. Her mother is of Norwegian and Ashkenazi Jewish heritage.

Clint Eastwood’s dating life has been complex throughout the decades. He dated Frances Fisher, a titanic-famed actress for six years in the 1990s and raised Francesco.

Her family resides on 17-Mile Drive in a home located over the Pebble Beach Golf Course. Because he prefers wide-open landscapes, Eastwood picked the Central Coast over Beverly Hills or New York City as his residence.

Education

When it comes to her education, she went to Robert Louis Stevenson School in Pebble Beach, California. After completing her school, she attended Morehouse College for further studies.

She moved to Los Angeles in 2013. and enrolled in the University of Southern California in Los Angeles to pursue her bachelor’s degree. 

Her Passion

Francesca is a very competent horse rider who has shown exceptional riding abilities since she was a child. 

She would devote many hours to honing her horseback riding skills, eventually competing in equestrian riding contests.

Born to be an Actor

Francesca had longed to be an actress since she was a child, so when she grew up, she chose to follow in her famous parents’ footsteps as an actor.

Despite the fact that Francesca is just 28 years old, she is already making waves in Hollywood. 

With seven half-siblings including sisters Kimber Tunis, Alison Eastwood, Kathryn Reeves, Laurie Murray, and Morgan, as well as brothers Kyle Eastwood and Scott Eastwood, only Francesca chose to be in the limelight.

She has an impeccable career that includes numerous critically praised films and television shows. And she doesn’t seem to be slowing down any time soon.

Mrs. Eastwood & Company, a reality show that aired on E!, featured her and her family on a whole new level. 

She has also been in TV shows such as Heroes Reborn, Fargo, and Twin Peaks: The Return, as well as films such as Jersey Boys and Old.

How did Francesca Eastwood’s Career Started?

When Francesca Eastwood was just two years old, she appeared in her mother’s movie titled The Stars Fell on Henrietta.

That was the moment when the future star grew up dreaming of one day becoming a successful actor, and the list of her achievements goes on and on.

Francesca played a role in True Crime, a thriller directed by and featuring her father, four years later. She wouldn’t perform on screen again for another 15 years, rejoining Clint on set for his 2014 film Jersey Boys, in which she played a waitress.

In 2012, the television reality show Mrs. Eastwood & Company aired on channel E! Francesca’s life, including those of her stepmother Dina Eastwood and half-sister Morgan Eastwood, was the subject of the reality documentary series.

At that time Francesca was dating Tyler Shields, who is a photographer. In one of its episodes, she and her boyfriend were caught on camera burning and damaging a $100,000 Hermès Birkin bag while photo-shooting.

All of the controversies resulted in death threats and other harsh reactions from fans and viewers of the show. 

“People spend $200,000 on an album cover, they spend millions on catering for movies, they spend money to produce things—that is all I am doing with this,” Shields remarked amid the uproar.

In 2013, Francesca Eastwood was named Miss Golden Globe 2013 by the Hollywood Foreign Press Association. The party was held at Cecconi’s restaurant in West Hollywood.

Francesca Eastwood gave a brief acceptance speech after receiving the award:

“I am honored to have been chosen by the Hollywood Foreign Press to be Miss Golden Globe of 2013. I have watched the Golden Globes ceremonies since I was a little girl, and it means so much to me to be a part of one of Hollywood’s most illustrious events.”

She was nineteen years old at the time and admitted to being worried about her debut when escorting winners on and off stage.

In 2017, she and her mother, Frances Fisher, played the young and old versions of the same character ‘Vivian Lord’ in the critically praised Fargo episode “The Law of Non-Contradiction.”

Private Life

Francesca and Jordan Feldstein, the brother of Beanie Feldstein and Jonah Hill, tied a knot on November 17, 2013 in Las Vegas. It was a small intimate wedding event.

Their relationship, however, was dissolved after only eight days and the reason is still not known.

Some media publications speculated in 2017 that she had married Clifton Collins Jr., although neither of them officially acknowledged the news.

Jordan Feldstein, Maroon 5’s manager, was found dead on December 22, 2017. According to the Los Angeles County Medical Examiner, he died abruptly as a result of a blood clot in his leg.

Pulmonary thromboembolism and deep leg vein thrombosis were the leading causes of death, as per a March 2018 coroner’s report.

Francesca made the announcement of her pregnancy with her mother, FIsher, at the 2018 Environmental Media Association Awards in May.

“I’m going to be adding to the family this summer with my own baby and we’re so excited,” Francesca said while caressing her pregnant belly.

In September 2018, Francesca and Alexander Wraith, a trainer and actor, welcomed their only child, Titan Wraith Eastwood.

For the love of Titan, her family hosted a baby shower at the Hotel Bel-Air in Los Angeles to welcome the new child.

The Father of Titan, Wraith keeps posting photographs of Titan on his Instagram account. But it’s unknown if the two are still dating.

Francesca Eastwood once posted pictures of her father holding her son, Titan, with the message, “Best dad. Best grandfather.”
