Ella Bleu Travolta Biography – Ella Bleu Travolta Wiki

Ella Bleu Travolta is an American actress and the daughter of John Travolta and Kelly Preston. As an actress, she is known for Old Dogs (2009), The Poison Rose (2019) and Entertainment Tonight (1981).

Ella made her movie debut in the 2009 movie, Old Dogs, along with her parents. American comedian actor, late Robin Williams was also featured in the movie. In 2018, she featured in the American biographical crime drama film, Gotti, again along her parents. Ella also landed a movie role in The Poison Rose. Her famous father also stars in the film.

Ella Bleu Travolta Parents

She is the daughter of John Travolta and Kelly Preston. Her parents got married in 1991. For her father John, it was his first marriage; and a second for her mother Kelly who was previously married in 1985 to Kevin Gage but later got divorced in 1987.

Ella Bleu Travolta Siblings
