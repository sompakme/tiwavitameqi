Carl Hagelin is a Swedish professional ice hockey player which has an estimated net worth of $2 million.

Carl Hagelin started his professional career when he played for two seasons with the J20 SuperElit's Södertälje. He went to the United States and appeared in the Michigan Wolverines. He led the team in winning the CCHA Championship during the 2009-2010 season.

Some of the recognition Hagelin received include CCHA Best Defensive Forward Award and finalist for the CCHA Player of the Year Award for the 2010-11 season. During the 2007 NHL Entry Draft, he was chosen in the sixth round by the New York Rangers.

Carl Oliver Hagelin was born on August 23, 1988 in Södertälje, Sweden.
