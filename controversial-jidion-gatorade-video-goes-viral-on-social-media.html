Controversial Jidion Gatorade Video Goes Viral On Social Media

In February 2023, a video went viral on social media in which an individual known as “Jidion” was seen tricking Gatorade employees into drinking “Prime Drink Ice Spice,” an energy drink.

The video, which was posted on Twitter, quickly gained attention from fans and celebrities alike, including YouTube stars KSI and Logan Paul.

This prank video was not the first time Jidion had caused controversy. He had previously starred in a controversial Super Bowl ad for Prime Drink Ice Spice, which showed him performing dangerous stunts, such as jumping off a building and setting himself on fire, to promote the drink.

The video quickly became a hot topic on social media, with many people expressing their opinions on the prank. KSI and Logan Paul, both of whom have a large following on YouTube, shared their reactions to the video.

While the video was a humorous prank, it removed KSI’s tweet and caused Gatorade to face criticism for its marketing tactics. KSI claimed that Gatorade had taken down his tweet promoting Jidion’s video using a DMCA claim.

This caused some to speculate that Gatorade was attempting to silence criticism of their brand.

Despite the controversy, Gatorade went ahead with featuring Prime in a Super Bowl ad, showing that they were not afraid to take risks in their marketing strategies.

Prime Drink Ice Spice continued to dominate a portion of the hydration market and was now going up against the likes of Gatorade.

Prime Hydration, the beverage created by former rivals KSI and Logan Paul, continues to fly off the shelves even a year after its initial launch. Many fans have not been able to get their hands on it.

It is believed that Prime already dominates a portion of the hydration market. With the rivalry between Prime Hydration and Gatorade continuing to heat up, it remains to be seen what is in store next for the brand.

In conclusion, the Jidion Gatorade video on Twitter caused a stir in the beverage industry, with many people expressing their opinions on the prank.

While it resulted in the removal of KSI’s tweet and caused Gatorade to face criticism for its marketing tactics, it did not stop Gatorade from featuring Prime in a Super Bowl ad.

Prime Hydration, created by KSI and Logan Paul, continues to be a success, dominating a portion of the hydration market and going up against the likes of Gatorade.
