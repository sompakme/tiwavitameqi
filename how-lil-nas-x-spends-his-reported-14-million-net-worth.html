How Lil Nas X Spends His Reported $14 Million Net Worth

Lil Nas X’s life turned upside down following the release of his smash hit “Old Town Road”, which was recently named the highest certified song in RIAA history, with sales of 15 million in the US and another four million worldwide.

In March 2019, the 22-year-old, who is best pals with singer Miley Cyrus, signed a lucrative deal with Columbia Records after an “intense” bidding war with a number of record labels, who had clearly seen how much of an impact the Georgia native had made on the charts.

Lil Nas X Bought His First House

In March 2021, it was revealed that the “Kick It” hitmaker had purchased a stunning $2.35 million mansion in Sherman Oaks, California — the news was later shared on his official Instagram page, where he confirmed having bought his first home.

According to Realtor, the property, which boasts 2,580 square feet, four bedrooms, and four bathrooms, was initially listed for $2.5 million in January but its seller reduced the price the following month by almost $150k, leading Nas X to make his offer.

Some of the high-end details include floating concrete steps over a large-sized waterfall that dribbles right into his pond. Inside, the singer-songwriter has an opening living and dining area which is anchored by a fireplace while glass pocket doors open out to a balcony with breathtaking views of the San Fernando Valley.

Lil Nas X's New Home Really Has It All

Other impressive features include Nas X’s enormous spa, a raised pool, plenty of storage room — presumably for all of the awards he’s accumulated in the past two years — while his master suite includes a private balcony and a custom bathroom with a free-standing tub and a rain shower.

"Bought my first house today,” Nas X captioned a series of collective images on Instagram from his new mansion in March. Another photo showed the star throwing a peace sign while holding on to a balloon with the message “congrats.”

His multi-million dollar purchase for a home in Sherman Oaks may be the priciest thing he’s spent some of his fortune on so far, but fans also shouldn’t forget the time Nas X splashed tens of thousands of dollars to surprise his “Old Town Road” collaborator with a brand new Maserati sports car.

Lil Nas X Gifted Billy Ray A Maserati

Billy later confirmed the report when he took to his Instagram and posted a photo of him and Nas posing in front of the expensive vehicle, along with an accompanying caption that read, “Driving down Rodeo in MY Maserati sports car! @lilnasx."

Nas X has certainly come a long way, having recently opened up about the depression and anxiety he faced just a year before releasing “OTR” and becoming an overnight sensation.

Lil Nas X Opens Up About His Struggles With Mental Health

He’d taken to TikTok earlier this year to share his story with fans, writing, "In 2017 I became the first [in] my family to get into college. During college, I was depressed, had no friends and…. my grandmother passed.”

He then developed hypochondria — an illness caused by anxiety — which caused him to be unduly alarmed about potentially having a serious medical condition without exhibiting any symptoms. "I started going to the doctor a lot in fear that I would die soon… hypochondria,” he added before mentioning that school wasn’t for him, so he decided to drop out.
