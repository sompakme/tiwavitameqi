Dont Worry About My Sister Who Has A Career

If there’s one celebrity entity that will ride for each other right or wrong until the end of time, it’s the Kardashian/Jenner clan. Khloe Kardashian is the latest to cape for her family on social media after catching wind of Amber Rose‘s latest sit-down with The Breakfast Club. After talking about her own personal life, Charlamagne Tha God asked Amber Rose for her thoughts on the ex of her bestie, Blac Chyna, dating a minor. Rose wagged her finger at Tyga courting the youngest Jenner (who is eight years his junior). “She’s a baby!” she said about Kylie. “She needs to go to bed at 7 o’clock and relax. He should be ashamed of himself. That’s how I feel. He has a beautiful woman and a baby and left that for a 16-year-old that just turned 17. That’s up to her and her mother, but I’m not with it.” Khloe didn’t take to kindly to the remarks and, without tagging anyone, countered Muva Rosebud’s arguments:

“@jaileenieweenie: “to provide for her family wyd” not mad she was a stripper. But dont say my sister should be in bed by 7 when she wasn’t

— Khloé (@khloekardashian) February 16, 2015

3 sides to EVERY story. His, hers and the truth. I’m not passing judgment but I wish others would do the same until they have FACTS.

— Khloé (@khloekardashian) February 16, 2015
