Who is Antoine Winfield Jr.? Antoine Winfield Jr. Biography and Wiki

Antoine Winfield Jr. is an American football player who plays as a defensive back for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League (NFL). Antonie is the son of a former Seattle Seahawks and Minnesota Vikings cornerback Antoine Winfield Sr. Winfield Jr was selected in the second round of the 2020 NFL Draft with the 45th pick by the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.
