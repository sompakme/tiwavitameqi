Von Gaskin Biography and Wiki

Von Gaskin is an American journalist who is a news anchor and reporter at WIS. Gaskin joined WIS in April of 2010 and is a proud native South Carolinian. She has been a meteorologist from the Lowcountry of SC to the Outer Banks of North Carolina.

Gaskin takes pride in working in her community and volunteering for local organizations and is also a member of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc. Formerly, Gaskin worked at WPDE from 2004 to 2006 as the weekend meteorologist.

In early June 2018, the Lake City native received an Emmy Award from the National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences Chapter. The Emmy was awarded to the WIS News Sunrise Crew for Hurricane Irma coverage. The WIS News Sunrise Crew received the Emmy for Best Daily Newscast during the coverage of Hurricane Irma. Gaskin was the meteorologist for that morning, updating viewers on the weather hazards associated with a land-falling tropical system, the release said.
