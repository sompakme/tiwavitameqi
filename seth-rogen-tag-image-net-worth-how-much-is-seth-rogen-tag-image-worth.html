Freaks and Geeks

One of Seth Rogen’s breakout roles came in the critically acclaimed television series “Freaks and Geeks.” Created by Paul Feig and produced by Judd Apatow, the coming-of-age dramedy quickly garnered a cult following. Rogen played the lovable and hilarious character Ken Miller, solidifying his ability to deliver humor and endearing performances.

Superbad

Seth Rogen’s role in the 2007 comedy film “Superbad” further elevated his career. Co-written by Rogen and his creative partner Evan Goldberg, the movie achieved both critical acclaim and commercial success. Rogen’s portrayal of Officer Michaels, a clueless police officer, showcased his comedic prowess and ability to bring laughter to audiences.

Pineapple Express

In 2008, Seth Rogen starred alongside James Franco in the action-comedy film “Pineapple Express.” Rogen’s performance as Dale Denton, a process server who finds himself embroiled in a dangerous drug conspiracy, demonstrated his versatility as an actor. The movie’s blend of comedy and thrilling action solidified Rogen’s status as a leading comedic actor in Hollywood.
