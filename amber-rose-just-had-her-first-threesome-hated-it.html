Amber Rose Just Had Her First Threesome, Hated It

Not so hot. Amber Rose revealed on her new Play.it podcast, Loveline With Amber Rose, that she recently had her first threesome and said that hooking up with two people at once was not the sexy experience she was hoping for. Hear what she said in the clip above!

“We kinda just got together. It was a guy and a girl and it was f‑‑king horrible,” the TV personality, 32, told cohost and sex therapist Dr. Chris Donaghue. “It was the worst. I am literally having sleepless nights.… I felt the pressure, getting older, to experience new things and I did it against my better judgment because I talk about sex a lot.”

Despite the allure of a ménage à trois, the How to Be a Bad Bitch author said that, ultimately, she finds that she prefers just one person underneath the sheets.

“And I hated it. Because I feel like I am a very passionate lover and I like that one-on-one passion,” the sex-positive star — who is gearing up to lead her second annual SlutWalk October 1 — shared. “With the threesome, I kind of felt like there was no passion. There was no kissing and rolling around, all of those moments that I enjoy. All those moments were not there.”

The former stripper then went on to crack a joke about asking her ex-husband, Wiz Khalifa (with whom she shares son Sebastian, 3), to join her in the bedroom next time she is feeling a little frisky.

“You know what? Maybe I’ll call my baby daddy tonight,” she quipped. “I won’t do it again. Maybe if it’s two guys, but not with another girl.”

During the Friday, August 5, episode of The Amber Rose Show, Rose (whose most famous other ex is Kanye West) explained that she is rather old-fashioned when it comes to her love life.

“I feel like I’m just such a nerd in the bedroom. People really think that I have orgies all day and, like, I do these crazy sex acts,” the newly minted Dancing With the Stars contestant said. “I’m like, ‘I’m really just a normal girl.'”
