BON Hotel Tripod Owerri, Owerri, Nigeria

Located in the city known as the entertainment capital of Nigeria, the BON Hotel Tripod Owerri offers great stays for both leisure and business travellers. The hotel offers 66 rooms of various types, including two presidential suites. 

The hotel is in close proximity to the Imo State Trade Investment Centre and the Imo State House of Assembly, making it ideally suited for both business travellers and parliamentarians.

With two conference facilities offering space for over 200 delegates, the hotel has a great deal to offer business travellers.

Those visiting Owerri for its entertainment offerings will find the hotel equally well located, and there are also some fun activities to enjoy with children.
