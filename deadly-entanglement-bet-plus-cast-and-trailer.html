Meet The Cast Of Deadly Entanglement on BET Plus

Deadly Entanglement cast consists of Monica Calhoun as Deidra, Memphis Cade as Charles, and Vanessa Simmons as Tammy in the lead role.

De’Variyay Harris as Jay, Rico Simonini as Zeeke, and Jessica A. Caesar as Pam will also be seen in the movie in the supporting role.

Monica Calhoun as Deidra

In the movie, Monica portrays the role of the toxic partner of Charles, who left his son and husband a few years back.

Deidra returns to the life of her son and former husband after years and starts claiming the teen son as her blood.

She has played the strongest and bold character in the movie, her performance is top-notch.

The American film and television actress Monica Calhoun was born and raised by her mother, Lorine in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

Calhoun moved to California to pursue her dream of becoming an actress in the late 1970s. She began acting in her early school days.

She went to the Los Angeles County High School for the Arts and started working professionally afterward.

The actress is renowned for Bagdad Cafe, The Players Club, The Salon, The Best Man, and The Best Man Holiday.

Memphis Cade as Charles

Memphis played the role of Charles in the movie. He is a loving husband and father to his wife and only son, Jay.

Everything is going well in his life until the arrival of Diedra and his past starts coming out in front of his wife.

Charles tried his best to save the family and be safe from the eye of Diedra, but it was not possible.

Memphis Cade is an actor and model based in Los Angeles, California, USA. He works under the Carrie Macy Talent Agency.

The model went to Alabama State University and graduated in Theatre Arts, Drama, and Dramatics in 2000.

In 2019, the popular actor Memphis joined Studio School and learned about acting and Screenwriting.

Memphis is famously known for his work in Boscoe’s The Shadow Man, Probable Cause, and Quietly and has also done several TV series.

Vanessa Simmons as Tammy

The famous American actress Vanessa played the lead character named Tammy. She is the wife of Charles and stepmom of Jay.

Tammy is a popular singer and lives happily with her husband and her son. She is unaware of what is coming in her life.

The renowned television personality Vanessa Simmons was born on August 5, 1983. She is the oldest among 6 siblings.

She graduated from St John’s University and started her career in the entertainment industry soon after graduating.

She gained popularity between 2005 and 2009 with her family in a famous MTV reality show Run’s House.

Some of her famous work includes Boogie Town, Dysfunctional Friends, The Caregiver, and A Holiday Change.

Deadly Entanglement Plot Synopsis

Deadly Entanglement Bet Plus movie tells the story of a middle-aged woman named Deidra. She is the ex-wife of Charles and mother to Jay.

She came out of nowhere to create chaos in everybody’s life as she wanted full custody of her teenage son.

Deidra left the family when her son was just a child. Nobody knew where she went and had almost forgotten about her presence.

On the other hand, Charles is married to a beautiful, talented, and loving wife named Tammy. They were very much happy with their life.

Tammy is the one who helped Charles to raise his and Deidra’s son Jay. She is close to Jay and loves him dearly.

Charles’s wife Tammy is also a talented singer in the movie, there are a few beautiful songs executed in this Deadly Entanglement movie.

After the return of Diadra, she started envying the beautiful and perfect life of his ex-husband.

She couldn’t resist the fact that her son is close to Tammy and was happy in the family. She then claimed his custody.

As the story progresses, things start getting messy, Diadra is cruel and is digging Charles’s past in front of his wife.

She could go to any length to make Charles and Tammy’s life miserable and gain her son back in her life.

Everything and everyone is connected with Deidra in the movie, who creates a great suspense and thrill to watch till the end.
