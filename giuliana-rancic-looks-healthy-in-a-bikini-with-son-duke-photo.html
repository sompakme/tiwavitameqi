Giuliana Rancic Looks Healthy in a Bikini With Son Duke: Photo

Happy and healthy! Giuliana Rancic showed off her bikini body while lounging with her 3-year-old son, Duke, in an Instagram post on Monday, April 18.

“Hanging in my favorite place with my favorite little guy,” she wrote of her only child with husband Bill Rancic. The Fashion Police host showed off her fit frame in a black swimsuit with a matching black visor and sunglasses.

It’s been quite some time since the Going Off Script author has bared her body after she was criticized throughout the 2015 awards season for her rail-thin appearance, which many believed was the result of an eating disorder.

Giuliana opened up to Today’s Savannah Guthrie in April 2015 and revealed that her weight loss was actually due to her cancer medication. The E! News star was diagnosed with breast cancer in 2011. “I do not have an eating disorder. I wanted to be very clear about that,” she told Guthrie. “In fact, I eat a lot. People always love to tweet: ‘Eat a burger.’ I eat burgers. It’s not working.”

The TV personality also spoke to Dr. Oz the same month about her struggle with skinny shaming. “Trust me, I don’t want to lose weight. I don’t think it looks attractive,” she said. “I think I used to be more attractive. But I’m doing the best I can.”
