Justin Bieber's Music Video Crush on Selena Gomez's Diss, Kissing Him

Justin Bieber‘s latest crush does not have the last name Gomez, and according to his new flame, it’s causing a bit of jealousy from his famous ex. Cailin Russo is the object of the superstar’s latest affections in his new “All That Matters” music video. And after he posted a photo of the blonde model kissing him on the cheek, the cat was out of the bag. 

Bieber’s ex Selena Gomez recently shared a selfie on Instagram with the caption, “I thought he only liked the Latina category. Smh… #nomakeup #hatewhengirlssaythat,” and Russo thinks the note was intentionally aimed at her. 

“It was clearly directed at him with me in the video, but like I said I was just doing my job,” Russo said in a new interview with Cosmopolitan. “Selena is a superstar and I’m just a girl in a video. She’s his ex, and I’m sure they still have feelings for each other.”

But anyone interested in acquiring the “Boyfriend” singer’s affections might have cause to be jealous. The 19-year-old video vixen reveals that they’re still in close communication. In fact, the singer is stored in Russo’s phone under an alias. 

“If my phone is out and I’m with people, I don’t want anyone to see if he texts me or FaceTimes me, ya know?” she said. 

But Russo reveals she does have a boyfriend, for the time being.
