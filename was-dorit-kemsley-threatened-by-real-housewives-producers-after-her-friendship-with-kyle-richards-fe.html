Highlights

  • Dorit Kemsley faced an ultimatum from RHOBH production to reveal her marriage's truth or risk demotion.
  • Kemsley's friendship with Kyle Richards deteriorated, leading to Richards feeling unsupported and untrusted.
  • Kemsley's actions, such as sharing a private text from Richards, further strained their friendship.

Season 14 of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills is currently underway after fans saw the Housewives filming at dinner, as reported by Instagram user @QueensOfBravo. More specifically, longtime friends and castmates Kyle Richards and Dorit Kemsley were seen arguing with one another. This comes after a rocky Season 13 reunion and rumors that Kemsley was being demoted by production.

In Season 13, fans saw Kemsley and Richards struggle with their friendship. Between Richards separating from her husband and creating a new bond with country star Morgan Wade and Kemsley dealing with her PTSD from a home invasion, the pair have drifted. With Kemsley’s lack of storylines and insincerity around her marriage, it’s rumored Kemsley was almost demoted. Continue reading to find out if Kemsley was threatened a demotion because of her failing friendship with Richards, the history of their problems, and where Kemsley stands today with Richards.

Was Dorit Kemsley Demoted Once Her Friendship With Kyle Richards Fell Apart?

In April 2024, RealityBlurb shared that production for RHOBH allegedly gave Dorit Kemsley an ultimatum: either share the truth about her crumbling marriage or she’ll be demoted to a friend of the series instead of a full-time Housewife. Although her friendship with Kyle Richards was flailing, production cared more about her honesty surrounding her marriage, like their tax issues and fidelity.

In Season 13, Kemsley’s marriage to Paul “PK” Kemsley was falling apart due to differing schedules and communication struggles. Fans saw the couple meet with marriage counselors to figure out their issues. And while Kemsley told Andy Cohen that her marriage was “stronger than ever,” the reality was different.

The Daily Mail reported that after months of living apart when PK was in London, he temporarily moved into the Beverly Hills Hotel amid marital issues. A source told the Daily Mail more about the ins and outs of their flawed marriage and how it would affect filming.

“[Dorit] wants to be a full-time cast member on the show, but she does not want to explain that she is on a break with PK and he has temporarily moved out of the house.”

The source continued, “She really does not want to be seen as a single mother in Beverly Hills, because once you are labeled that you don’t get invited to as many parties, you lose friends, you are an insider.”

Exposing the highs and lows of her marriage could make things worse for the couple, but losing out on being a Housewife would be a major shift for Kemsley.

If production gave Kemsley an ultimatum, they got their wish when she announced her separation from PK. In a statement on Instagram, Kemsley wrote, “We as a couple have been subject to a lot of speculation about our marriage, We have had our struggles over the last few years and continue to work through them as two people who love each other and share two amazing children together."

"To safeguard our deep friendship and maintain a harmonious environment for our children we have made the mutual and difficult decision to take some time apart and re-evaluate our relationship while we prioritize our children."

Kemsley ended the statement by saying “We appreciate your love and support while we continue to do the work necessary throughout this journey.”

Dority Kemsley And Kyle Richards' Friendship Has Slowly Been Fizzling Out

Although Kyle Richards and Dorit Kemsley used to be close friends, their friendship has been dwindling since Season 12. When Richards had ongoing issues with her older sister Kathy Hilton, she was upset that Kemsley didn't support her — especially at the Season 12 reunion, as reported by E! Online. After Richards saw Kemsley's true colors, things were on a downward spiral from there.

Kyle Richards Didn't Feel Supported by Dorit Kemsley At Season 12 Reunion

In Season 12, Richards and her older sister Kathy Hilton butted heads. Things came to a head when the Housewives traveled to Aspen for a getaway. E! Online shared that when the women were out for the night, Hilton and Richards got into a screaming match, which altered the future of their relationship. At the reunion, Richards felt defeated by her sister and looked to Kemsley for support. Instead, Kemsley stayed out of it. "I have a relationship with Kathy, which was independent of my relationship with you. I didn't want to get in the middle," Kemsley said to Richards. Richards was disappointed in Kemsley since they were far closer than Kemsley was with Hilton.

Kyle Claimed Dorit 'Exaggerated' Their Friendship

Over the years, Richards and Kemsley have joined forces with other Housewives Lisa Rinna and Erika Jayne as an alliance against the other Housewives. As time went on, however, it appears Richards didn't see the value in their friendship the same way Kemsley did. According to Reality Blurb, Kemsley was upset that Richards didn't invite her on any of her vacations like she used to when they were close. Richards, however, fired back that they only did vacations together for the show. "Let’s not exaggerate the situation," Richards said.

Kyle Felt Dorit's Questions About Her Marriage Were Disingenuous

In Season 13, Kemsley knew things weren't great in Richards's marriage but was confused as to why Richards wasn't confiding in her. With the Bravo cameras rolling, Kemsley asked her deeper questions about her relationship and how things were going because she read breakup rumors in the tabloids. Richards didn't appreciate Kemsley's meddling and voiced her frustrations to Bravo.

Dorit Stirred The Pot Around Kyle And Morgan Wade's Friendship

Kemsley continued stirring the pot in Richards' love life in Season 13 once rumors circulated that Richards was romantically linked to her new friend, country singer Morgan Wade. Instead of allowing Richards to grieve the loss of her marriage and entertain a new friendship, Kemsley instigated and fed into the narrative that Richards was now dating a woman. According to People, Richards said, "You knew that by saying something like that, it was going to create a bigger problem for me in the media with the whole Morgan situation."

Dorit Shared A Private Text From Kyle With The Cast.

One of the final nails in the coffin was when Kemsley shared a private text from Richards on the day of the Season 13 reunion. Richards texted Kemsley hoping to keep the issues they had in their friendship private. Instead, Kemsley shared the message with a few women in the cast. Kemsley felt that Richards was trying to "silence" her. Richards was shocked that Kemsley shared her private message with production, causing even more trust issues.

Now that Dorit Kemsley and Kyle Richards are going through public separations while in a tumultuous place in their friendship, Richards gave an update on where they stood.

In an interview with US Weekly, Richards said she and Kemsley were "not great." “The dynamics have shifted tremendously this year," Richards confided. And while they're going through similar situations, the support system they once had with each other is no longer there.

Despite their hot and cold friendship, Dorit Kemsley was able to remain a full-time Housewife by giving production what they were looking for: an honest look at her marriage and the breakdown of her friendship with Richards.
