Contract Signing, Logan Paul, Womens Tag Title Match Set For 1/19 WWE SmackDown

WWE is loading up the card for the January 19 episode of WWE SmackDown.

As announced on the January 12 episode, Roman Reigns, Randy Orton, AJ Styles, and LA Knight will sign the contract for their title match at WWE Royal Rumble. Reigns will defend the Undisputed WWE Universal Championship against all three men.

Plus, after WWE United States Champion Logan Paul threatened to beat Kevin Owens in a court of law, Owens invited him to appear on The KO Show. The segment was confirmed for next week’s show. Owens will challenge Paul for the gold at WWE Royal Rumble.

Finally, Alba Fyre and Isla Dawn will challenge Katana Chance and Kayden Carter for the WWE Women’s Tag Team Championship.

Updated Card For 1/19 WWE SmackDown

The announced card for the January 19 episode of WWE SmackDown is as follows:

  • WWE Royal Rumble contract signing with Roman Reigns, LA Knight, Randy Orton, and AJ Styles
  • The KO Show with Logan Paul
  • : Katana Chance and Kayden Carter (c) vs. Alba Fyre & Isla Dawn

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs on January 19.
