Nikko Gonzalez is the son of both Lauren Sanchez and ex-NFL star, Tony Gonzalez

Once again, little is known about Sanchez's relationship with Whitesell, but, according to Style, it's rumored that Whitesell was actually the one who introduced Sanchez to Bezos.

It's unclear if Whitesell and Sanchez are on positive terms, but it is clear that she and her first husband, Tony Gonzalez have a very positive relationship. Gonzalez has been spotted a few times on double dates with his ex and Jeff Bezos. He's also been invited to various parties thrown by Bezos, including his 2021 New Year's Eve party aboard his yacht in Saint Barts.

In one image from the party, Lauren Sanchez's eldest son Nikko can be spotted with both his NFL legend father and future step-father. This positive bond obviously suggests that Nikko is a fan of his mother's extremely rich man.

While Nikko Gonzalez doesn't post a lot on his Instagram, the few images he has reveal a lot about his day-to-day life. This includes an image of him lying shirtless at the bottom of an empty pool on a yacht deck (which is presumably one of Bezos' own mega-yachts).

Nikko's Instagram depicts him as an often-shirtless, rich boy, happy to be enjoying the lavish life that he was brought up with. This includes images of him casually hanging out at fancy restaurants around the world, including in Paris, France, swimming with sharks in Bora Bora, climbing waterfalls in Maui wearing nothing but his underwear, and partying on Jeff Bezos' mega-yacht in Saint Barts.

Nikko also appears to be an avid skier, or, at the very least, a talented one. In a post featuring what looks like his stepbrothers on Jeff Bezos' side, Nikko can be seen jetting down a mountain in Yellowstone Park with the grace and agility of a pro skier.
