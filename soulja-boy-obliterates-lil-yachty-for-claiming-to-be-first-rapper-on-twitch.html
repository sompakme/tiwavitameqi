Soulja Boy Obliterates Lil Yachty For Claiming To Be First Rapper On Twitch

Soulja Boy lashed at Lil Yachty after he claimed to be the first rapper on Twitch. On Saturday (Oct. 14), Yachty made the claim when speaking with Cool Kicks’ YouTube channel. “I was the first rapper to stream,” he claimed. “2017. Before anyone. Twitch wasn’t even popping. I was on Twitch. It was me and Post Malone on Twitch. 2017, nobody was there. I think I was there before Post Malone, but it was just us as the only rappers. Seven years ago.” 

Soulja Boy caught wind of the comments and responded to Lil Boat’s claim. He began his rant by verbally attacking the Atlanta native. Soulja then dragged Drake into the mix, calling the men out for painting their toe nails. 

“I ain’t gon’ even cap. Lil Yachty, sit yo bi**h-a** down, ni**a,” he said. “Drake, you and Lil Yachty getting on my muthaf**king nerves. F**k wrong with y’all pu**y a** ni**as? Lil Yachty, you is a bi**h nig**a. Fruity-a** ni**a. Painting yo muthaf**king toe nails and sh*t. Bi**h, you wasn’t the first ni**a to do sh*t. Lil bi**h a**, pu**y a**, punk a**, Oreo-looking a**, f**k-a** ni**a.”

“I’m getting tired of y’all f**k ni**as. Lil bi**h-a** ni**a. Lil Black a** ni**a,” he added. “Lil Yachty, sit your lil’ pu**y a** down. Bi**h a** ni**a. You wasn’t the first ni**a to do sh*t. I’m getting tired of y’all pu**y a** ni**as, man.”

VIBE Check: While Yachty claims to have been the first rapper, Soulja started his Twitch account before the ATL rapper. According to Social Blade statistics, the “Crank Dat” entertainer began streaming on the video game platform on Dec. 16, 2016.

In fact, the same social metrics have determined that Yachty actually began his channel on March 3, 2018 — two years after Soulja Boy. Post Malone started his Twitch on March 5, 2018. So, unless Boat has another channel he’s referring to, it turns out he wasn’t the first rapper on Twitch, and neither was Posty.
