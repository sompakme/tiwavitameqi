Scottish-American actor Alan Cumming has an estimated net worth of $5 million. Throughout his entire career as an actor, Cumming has already appeared in numerous plays, TV shows and films. In fact, he has appeared in stage shows like Accidental Death of an Anarchist, The Bacchae, Bent and Hamlet. He has also appeared in Broadway plays such as Design for Living, The Threepenny Opera, and Cabaret.

Alan Cumming was born on January 27, 1965 in Aberfeldy, Perthshire, Scotland. Since 2007, he has been married to Grant Shaffer. He is also known to be a promoter of same-sex marriage, LGBT rights, and foreskin preservation. He is known for his AIDS charities and has expressed his ideas against routine infant circumcision.

As an actor, he debuted in the 1986 film Passing Glory. Six years later, he starred in a film titled Prague. Among his acting projects, which won him awards including Accidental Death of an Anarchist, Bernard and the Genie, Prague, Hamlet, Cabaret, and The Anniversary Party.
