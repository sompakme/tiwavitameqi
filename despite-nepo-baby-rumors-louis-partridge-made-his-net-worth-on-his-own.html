Despite Nepo Baby Rumors, Louis Partridge Made His Net Worth On His Own

Highlights

  • Louis Partridge is not a product of nepotism.
  • Partridge's family has been supportive of his career.
  • Partridge is in a romantic relationship with Olivia Rodrigo.

Louis Partridge gained worldwide recognition when he landed the role opposite Millie Bobby Brown in Netflix's Enola Holmes. Despite their on-screen chemistry in the movie and its sequel, Partridge and Brown maintained a low-profile relationship and never actually dated.

Like his girlfriend, Olivia Rodrigo, who takes pride in her ethnicity and Filipino roots, Partridge relied on his talents and worked diligently from a young age to attain the current level of success he experienced in the entertainment industry.

Is Louis Partridge A Nepo Baby?

Louis Partridge doesn't come from a family with ties to the entertainment business; in other words, he is not a product of nepotism.

In an interview with Glass Magazine, Partridge shared:

"My dad always wanted to be an actor, so I sometimes feel like he's living vicariously through me. But yeah, he didn't become an actor. I think his mum said to him: 'You won't earn a penny. You need a proper job.' But I was very much supported. My parents aren't actors at all."

Despite his family's unfamiliarity with the entertainment industry, Partridge's parents and two sisters have been very supportive of his career. They attended Partridge's movie premiere to cheer him on. In July 2022, Partridge shared a photo on Facebook showcasing his family standing by him at a red carpet event for Pistol, with the caption "Family love" and a red emoji.

How Much Is Louis Partridge's Net Worth?

According to The Richest, Louis Partridge amassed a $2 million net worth through his contributions to a series of movies, while other sources report his net worth to be $1.5 million.

When asked about his dream acting role, Partridge told The Glass that he envisioned a coming-of-age film:

"My dream role would be in a coming-of-age film, something centered around teenagers or young adults but with a raw and authentic perspective. I want to venture into something different from what I've done before. It's easy to stick to what you know, but I aspire to take on a role that challenges me as an actor."

When Did Louis Partridge Become Olivia Rodrigo's Boyfriend?

During a game of 17 questions for Seventeen Magazine back in 2020, Louis Partridge revealed that he was single but confessed that he had previously experienced heartbreak. In 2022, there were speculations about Partridge being in a relationship with his Pistol co-star, Sydney Chandler, but their relationship was never officially confirmed.

In October 2023, Partridge was seen in London with Grammy Award winner Olivia Rodrigo. Images shared by fans on social media depicted them appearing quite comfortable with each other. One photo showed Partridge and Rodrigo with arms wrapped around each other's waists, while in another, Rodrigo sported festive Halloween-inspired cat ears, smiling at Partridge. They also posed for selfies with numerous fans while discreetly strolling in public.
