GCW Officially Announces Dates And Venue For The Collective 2023

The Collective will follow WrestleMania to Los Angeles in 2023.

Just as ran The Collective in Dallas alongside WrestleMania in 2022, the weekend-long series of events will be held in “The City of Angels” next year.

GCW announced the news in a tweet on April 3 and revealed that The Collective will be held from March 30 to April 1, 2023. The The Ukranian Cultural Center in Los Angeles, California, will be the host venue.

*BREAKING*

The Collective returns for Mania Week 2023 and we're bringing it to our home base in LOS ANGELES!

The Collective 2023
March 30th – April 1st
The Ukranian Cultural Center
Los Angeles, CA

Joey Janela’s Spring Break, Josh Barnett’s Bloodsport, For The Culture, Effy’s Big Gay Brunch are a few examples of the many shows that are held at The Collective, which usually airs on FITE.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
